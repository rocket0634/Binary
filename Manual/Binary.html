Keep Talking and Nobody Explodes Mod Binary

On the Subject of Binary

Strange, but these codes are easy and impossible at the same time.

This module consist of screen and five buttons: "0", "1", "R", "Send" and "Useless button".

On the screen you will see the word. Translate each letter of the word to binary with table below and submit it back with "0" and "1" buttons. To submit your input press "send" button. You can also press "R" button to reset your input.

If buttons are red, screen doesn`t show a word or a screen is pressable, you are looking at a different module.
