Keep Talking and Nobody Explodes Mod Broken Binary

On the Subject of Broken Binary

Or binordered keys, if you prefer.

This module consist of five buttons. Each of three top small buttons are cycling through 8 digits, spelling out a letter. Bottom buttons also have letters written on them. Each letter encrypted with ASCII binary code. Decrypted letters gives scrambled word. Unscramble word and submit it pressing corresponding buttons.

If screen show a word, you are looking at a different module.
